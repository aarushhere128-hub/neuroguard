What does this test check?

During the FAST stroke assessment, difficulty raising one arm or one arm drifting downward may indicate possible muscle weakness. This AI assessment is designed only for educational screening and is NOT a medical diagnosis. If you suspect a stroke, seek emergency medical care immediately.
