Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

NeuroGuard is an educational prototype developed to increase awareness of stroke symptoms using the FAST (Face, Arm, Speech, Time) assessment. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled while using the application.

2. Information We Collect

The application may temporarily process:

  • Camera input for facial and arm movement assessment.
  • Microphone input for speech analysis.
  • Symptom onset time entered by the user.
  • Basic interaction data within the application.

NeuroGuard does not require users to create an account or provide personally identifiable information.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

  • Demonstrate AI-assisted stroke screening.
  • Perform FAST assessment simulations.
  • Provide educational guidance.
  • Record symptom onset time for demonstration purposes.

4. Data Storage

The current version of NeuroGuard is an educational prototype. Assessment data is processed locally whenever possible and is not permanently stored or shared.

5. Camera and Microphone Access

Camera and microphone permissions are requested only for FAST assessment features. Users may deny these permissions, although related features may not function.

6. Data Sharing

NeuroGuard does not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties.

7. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information processed by the application. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Medical Disclaimer

NeuroGuard is an educational prototype and is not a medical device. It does not diagnose stroke or replace professional medical advice.

If stroke symptoms are suspected, immediately contact your local emergency medical services.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the project develops. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date above.
